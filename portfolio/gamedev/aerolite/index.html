Aerolite Entertainment is an international indie game development team that focuses on creating 3D games in Unreal Engine.
We have been working on games since 2021, utilizing softwares such as Perforce, Unreal Engine, Maya, zBrush, Adobe programs like Substance, and more. We have been able to construct games with the purpose of honing our skills.

Team size reached so far: 15+

Members from around the world: USA, Europe, Africa, India, UK, Switzerland, Italy
